WAVE: The Official Aero Club of SPCE, Mumbai

ABOUT SPCE

Bharatiya Vidya Bhavan's Sardar Patel College Of Engineering (SPCE), one of the highest-ranking colleges in Maharashtra, is a Government-aided autonomous college established in 1962. Over the last 50 years, the college has gained an excellent reputation in technical education and has received a Grade 'A' rating from the Government of Maharashtra.

About WAVE

WAVE is a student organization established in 2017, bringing together students who are passionate about aeromodelling and aerodynamics. Our club focuses on the development of model aircraft and rockets, providing hands-on experience that complements academic learning.
We begin by conceptualizing and visualizing different models while analyzing their aerodynamic properties. This foundation leads to the design, optimization, and manufacturing phases. We focus on creating a learning environment where technical knowledge and practical experience are passed forward to future students, ensuring continuous growth and innovation within our club.

Vision

  • To represent India across the globe at international aero-modelling competitions and bring laurels to the country.
  • To spread knowledge about UAVs and ignite passion within the youth.

Mission

  • To build more efficient aircraft while spreading UAV knowledge amongst youngsters.
  • To innovate new designs for implementation in the industry.
